In contrast to current chatbots, like Cleverbot or Evie, a chatbot with a cognitive ability would understand what it was saying, rather than just outputing something based off of its algorithm. While I don't believe this will be accomplished by me or even in my lifetime, I believe research into this topic would lead to other, more advanced, forms of Artificial Intelligence.
